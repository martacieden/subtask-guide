# Документація онбординг завдань

## Головна онбординг таска

**Назва:** Welcome to NextGen — Your Quick Start Guide

**Опис (відображається в секції Description):**
```
Welcome!

We've created a few quick tasks to help you get started. Complete them in any order—they'll take about 5 minutes total. Mark each subtask complete as you go. Need help? Click the [?] icon or visit our Help Center
```

**Категорія:** Onboarding  
**Проект:** Onboarding  
**Статус:** Created  
**Пріоритет:** Normal

---

## Сабтаска 1: Review your homepage

**Назва:** Review your homepage—this is where your assigned items appear

**Опис:**
Your homepage is the central hub where you'll see all tasks, decisions, and items assigned to you. This is where you'll start your work each day and track what needs your attention.

**Що ви навчитеся:**
You'll learn how to navigate your personal dashboard, understand the layout, and see where your assigned work appears.

**Навігація:**
- Кнопка "Перейти до..." → веде на Homepage (/)

**Пошагові інструкції:**
1. Navigate to the homepage using the 'Home' link in the left sidebar
2. Review the Tasks section to see your assigned items
3. Check the Decisions section for items requiring your input
4. Notice how items are organized by status and priority

---

## Сабтаска 2: Check the left navigation

**Назва:** Check the left navigation to see Decisions, Projects, and other modules

**Опис:**
The left navigation panel is your gateway to all modules in Way2B1. Each module serves a specific purpose in managing your organization's workflow.

**Що ви навчитеся:**
You'll understand the structure of the platform, learn about different modules (Decisions, Projects, Tasks, Budgets, Catalog), and how to navigate between them.

**Підсвітка:**
- Кнопка "Показати елемент" → підсвічує елемент з id="navigation" (ліва навігаційна панель)

**Пошагові інструкції:**
1. Look at the left sidebar - you'll see icons for different modules
2. Click on 'Decisions' to see decision management
3. Click on 'Projects' to see project tracking
4. Click on 'Tasks' to see task management
5. Each module has its own purpose and features

---

## Сабтаска 3: Leave a comment

**Назва:** Leave a comment on this task (By the way, you can @mentioning someone)

**Опис:**
Comments are a powerful way to collaborate on tasks. You can add notes, ask questions, and use @mentions to notify specific team members.

**Що ви навчитеся:**
You'll learn how to add comments, use @mentions to tag team members, and understand how collaboration works in tasks.

**Підсвітка:**
- Кнопка "Показати елемент" → підсвічує елемент з id="comment-input"
- Автоматичний скрол до секції "comments"

**Пошагові інструкції:**
1. Scroll down to the Comments section on the right side
2. Click in the comment input field
3. Type your comment
4. Use @ followed by a name to mention someone (e.g., @John Doe)
5. Click the Send button to post your comment

---

## Сабтаска 4: Change task status

**Назва:** Try changing this task's status using the dropdown

**Опис:**
Task status helps track progress. You can change status from 'Created' to 'In Progress', 'Review', or 'Done' to reflect where the task is in your workflow.

**Що ви навчитеся:**
You'll learn how to update task status, understand the different status options, and see how status changes affect task visibility and organization.

**Підсвітка:**
- Кнопка "Показати елемент" → підсвічує елемент з id="task-status"
- Автоматичний скрол до секції "details"

**Пошагові інструкції:**
1. Scroll to the Details section
2. Find the 'Status' field
3. Click on the status dropdown
4. Select a new status (e.g., 'In Progress')
5. Notice how the status badge updates immediately

---

## Сабтаска 5: Explore modules

**Назва:** Explore one module that interests you (Decisions, Projects, etc.)

**Опис:**
Each module in Way2B1 has unique features. Take time to explore modules that are relevant to your role - whether it's Decisions for approvals, Projects for tracking, or Budgets for financial planning.

**Що ви навчитеся:**
You'll discover the capabilities of different modules, understand which ones are most relevant to your work, and learn how to use them effectively.

**Підсвітка:**
- Кнопка "Показати елемент" → підсвічує елемент з id="navigation"

**Пошагові інструкції:**
1. Click on any module in the left sidebar that interests you
2. Explore the interface - notice the layout and features
3. Try creating a new item if there's a 'New' button
4. Review existing items to understand the structure
5. Navigate back to Tasks when you're done exploring

## Сабтаска 6: Mark task complete

**Назва:** Mark this task complete when you're ready

**Опис:**
Once you've completed all the onboarding steps and feel comfortable with the platform, mark this task as complete. This signals that you're ready to start using Way2B1 for your real work.

**Що ви навчитеся:**
You'll learn how to mark tasks as complete, understand when tasks should be marked done, and see how completion affects your task list.

**Підсвітка:**
- Кнопка "Показати елемент" → підсвічує елемент з id="task-status"
- Автоматичний скрол до секції "details"

**Пошагові інструкції:**
1. Go to the Details section
2. Find the Status dropdown
3. Select 'Done' from the dropdown
4. The task will be marked as complete
5. You'll see a celebration when all onboarding steps are done!

## Загальна інформація

**Орієнтовний час виконання:** 5-6 хвилин

**Порядок виконання:** Завдання можна виконувати в будь-якому порядку

**Особливості:**
- Кожна сабтаска має власну детальну сторінку з breadcrumbs
